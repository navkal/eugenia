My interests range widely, from nonprofit work to social entrepreneurship to writing, and more. Here are a few of the things I spend my time on.

  • Field Training Program


    How do we prepare for crucial research work abroad? Currently, there is no curriculum or training program in existence that develops the situational awareness skills that young professionals need with how to conduct effective qualitative and quantitative field work abroad. With the Foreign Policy Research Institute and David Danelo, I've developed a curriculum and train young professionals and students interested in doing research in dangerous or politically unstable climates. We train on security, situational awareness, field work methods, and storytelling.

  • LEAP


    In 2012, in partnership with a local community-based organization called Lepta Community, I began working with a year-long leadership development program called LEAP. This program is geared towards underprivileged but promising young leaders from the Mathare slum. I taught weekly classes on social entrepreneurship, developing creativity, innovation, and change. Currently, I offer curriculum support, provide mentorship and insights where necessary, and advise on strategic growth.
