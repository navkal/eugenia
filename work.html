Testimonials

In August 2013, I began consulting with Titagya Schools, a nonprofit based working in rural villages in Ghana. Titagya had just trained 27 teachers on individualized teaching and student engagement, and were trying to understand how to assess the success of the training and implementation. This was Titagya’s first attempt to understand the impact of their training program.

I worked with Titagya to develop a strategy for effective follow-up and understanding impact. I created a guide for effective interviewing, a process document, and a questionnaire that would ask about the key points of the training and assess teacher comprehension.
